## Research and Teaching Associate, Department of Management
**The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen** · Jan 2018 – Jan 2022
*Supervisor: Dr. Xiao Fenglong*

* Led data governance, econometric modelling support, and variable construction for a longitudinal research programme on board-level political embeddedness, financial disclosure quality, and risk transparency.
* Assembled and systematised multi-year, firm-level panel datasets from CSMAR and Wind for Chinese A-share listed manufacturing enterprises.
* Operationalised corporate nonmarket strategy measures, including political ties through National People's Congress and Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference delegations and executives' government backgrounds.
* Supported empirical analyses addressing endogeneity concerns through panel fixed-effects models, instrumental-variable estimations, and propensity-score matching.
* Prepared literature reviews on authoritarian state–business relations and institutional transitions to support theoretical framing for draft chapters and research manuscripts.

## Research Associate, National Social Science Foundation Key Project
**Shenzhen University** · Apr 2022 – Dec 2024
*Supervisor: Prof. Zeng Xiaosong*

* Conducted field-based comparative research on cross-border transmission, regional adoption, and institutional variation across trade-network societies, with fieldwork in Belt and Road recipient countries including Indonesia and Sri Lanka.
* Constructed and managed a multi-country institutional database, translating heterogeneous regulatory and documentary sources into structured research variables where no comparable form existed across regions.
* Developed item-level document coding protocols and conducted inter-coder reliability assessment using Cohen's κ on bilingual policy, legal, and archival texts.
* Coordinated cross-border project operations, compiled annual academic expenditure audits, and managed communications with government funding agencies.
